📚 Financial Knowledge Vault

The "Why" behind every metric — because understanding beats guessing

⚖️

Why 50% FOIR?

The Indian banking safety ceiling. RBI guidelines and CRISIL data show households with FOIR above 50% have 3× higher default probability. Banks use this to protect both you and themselves.

FOIR = (Total EMIs ÷ Gross Income) × 100
📉

Why 6% Inflation?

India's CPI inflation averages 5.5–6.5% annually. The RBI targets 4% ±2%. Using 6% is conservative planning. At 6%, your money halves in purchasing power every 12 years.

Future Cost = P × (1 + 0.06)ⁿ
🔄

Why Reducing Balance?

Interest is calculated on the outstanding principal, not the original loan amount. This is why prepaying early has outsized impact — you reduce the base on which future interest is calculated.

EMI = P × r(1+r)ⁿ / ((1+r)ⁿ-1)
📊

The 50/30/20 Rule

Popularized by Senator Elizabeth Warren. Needs (50%) = rent + utilities + EMIs. Wants (30%) = dining, travel, lifestyle. Savings (20%) = emergency fund, investments, SIPs.

Needs ≤ 50% | Wants ≤ 30% | Savings ≥ 20%
🏦

Processing Fee & GST

A 2% processing fee on ₹10L = ₹20,000. GST at 18% on that fee = ₹3,600. You pay ₹23,600 at disbursement but receive ₹9,76,400. Your effective interest rate is therefore higher than stated.

Disbursal = P − (Fee + 18% GST on Fee)
🎯

SIP vs Debt — The Opportunity Cost

Nifty 50 CAGR since 1999 is ~14%. Using a conservative 12% for planning, a SIP often accumulates more than the loan cost. But loans enable goals now. The choice depends on urgency vs wealth-building.

SIP Corpus = P × ((1+r)ⁿ − 1) / r × (1+r)
🚨

Credit Hunger Warning

Each hard enquiry drops your CIBIL score by 5–10 points and stays for 2 years. 3+ enquiries in 6 months signals "credit hunger" to banks — a red flag that makes approvals harder and rates higher.

Score Impact ≈ −(enquiries × 7) points
🛡️

Emergency Fund Rule

The standard rule: 6 months of total fixed obligations (rent + EMIs + utilities). In India, with a volatile job market and fewer social safety nets, 9–12 months is recommended for salaried professionals.

Target = (Rent + All EMIs) × 6
